About Ethan

I approach software as a mix of problem-solving, hands-on learning, and iteration. I like building projects that give me something concrete to improve, whether that means a cleaner interface, better structure, or stronger implementation decisions.

Witty’s Website reflects that approach. It is meant to feel like a personal developer hub rather than a plain resume page, showing both finished work and the growth that happens behind it.

What I value in my work

I care about building useful projects, learning from mistakes, and organizing work in a way that becomes easier to maintain over time. Each new build is a chance to strengthen both technical and creative judgment.

I want my portfolio to show progress honestly: where I started, what I can build now, and how I’m continuing to improve.
